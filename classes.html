What makes S-Tier in Season 12?

S-Tier classes dominate S12 through a combination of multiple viable S-tier builds, strong performance across all content types (speed farming, boss killing, group, solo), and forgiving playstyles that work with imperfect gear. Paladin claims the top spot with the devastating Auradin build (holy aura stacking = passive damage + defense), while Spiritborn follows close behind as the easiest and fastest speed farmer with Eagle Infinite Evade trivializing Helltide and Nightmare Dungeons.

Lore

Class Lore Briefs

🏋️

Barbarian

Nomadic wanderers of Sanctuary's harsh Dry Steppes, Barbarians are battle-hardened survivors who draw their fury from the rage within. Descended from the ancient Druids who refused to take shelter when the Worldstone shattered, they carry on the old ways — unbroken, unbowed, and utterly relentless in combat.

🐺

Druid

Guardians of Scosglen's ancient druid order, Druids are shapeshifters who draw power from nature's primal spirits. They commune with the land itself — calling upon earth, storm, and the spirits of beast and tree. Their order dates back to the very founding of Sanctuary, when Inarius and Lilith first created the nephalem.

💀

Necromancer

Priests of Rathma — the unspoken god of death — Necromancers walk the fine line between life and death. They harness the power of blood, bone, and shadow, commanding the dead not as servants but as extensions of the natural cycle. Their order predates the Cathedral and the Light, rooted in the oldest truths of Sanctuary.

⚔️

Paladin

Reborn outcasts from a shattered holy order, Paladins are Wardens of Light who carry the flame of righteousness through a world that has forgotten it. After the original Cathedral of Light fell to darkness, the surviving faithful forged a new path — one tempered by suffering and hardened by exile, yet burning with renewed purpose.

🗡️

Rogue

Wanderers, bounty hunters, and survivors of Sanctuary's harshest lands, Rogues answer to no order and no creed. Masters of shadows and blades, they survive by their wits and their steel. Found in every region — from the fog-shrouded forests of Scosglen to the sun-bleached ruins of Kehjistan — they are the freelancers of Sanctuary's endless war.

🔥

Sorcerer

Wielders of elemental magic drawn from the fundamental forces of the world, Sorcerers channel fire, ice, and lightning through sheer will and ancient knowledge. Whether trained in the academies of Kehjistan or as hedge-witches in the wilds, their power is unmatched — and their fragility a constant reminder that knowledge without armor can be a fatal weakness.

🦅

Spiritborn

The apex predators of Nahantu's corruption-infested jungle, Spiritborn are warriors who bond with the great spirit guardians of the jungle — the Eagle, the Jaguar, the Centipede, and the Gorilla. Their ancient order has defended Nahantu for generations, wielding spirit synergies that turn the jungle itself into a weapon. Now their homeland faces its greatest threat yet.

🌑

Warlock

Coming in Lord of Hatred (April 28, 2026). The Warlock class will enter Sanctuary wielding the forbidden arts of demon pact and shadow hexes. As the Lord of Hatred expansion approaches and Mephisto stirs in his ancient prison, the dark powers that once threatened to consume Sanctuary may find new champions among those desperate or foolish enough to wield them.

🌑 Warlock — Coming April 28, 2026

Lord of Hatred Expansion

The Darkest Class Yet

The Warlock launches alongside the Lord of Hatred expansion on April 28, 2026 — and from what has been revealed so far, it is unlike anything Sanctuary has seen before. Built around demon summoning, shadow hexes, and a dual-resource system (Wrath + Dominance), the Warlock is a caster-summoner hybrid that turns the enemy's darkness into your greatest weapon.

🎯 4 Archetypes: Legions (Summoner), Vanguards (Demonform), Masterminds (Shadow Hexes), Ritualists (Blood Rites)
🩸 New Keywords: Hex, Eviscerate, Shadowform, Demonform, Volatility, Soul Shards
⚔️ Great Demon: Each archetype binds a unique Greater Demon that defines your playstyle
📦 Soul Shards: Unlocks at Level 30 — transformative passive + demon binding system
