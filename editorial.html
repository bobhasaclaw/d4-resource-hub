🖋️ Editorial Standards

How the newsroom works

The editorial side of D4 Resource Hub is built to help players move faster: official-source first, short summaries, and clear next steps instead of recycled news noise.

Editorial Model

The newsroom prioritizes official Blizzard sources first, then summarizes what those updates mean for real players. The goal is to shorten the path from announcement to action.

What gets covered

The strongest candidates are official announcements, patch notes, expansion updates, season launch details, event posts, and recap material that helps explain Blizzard’s direction. The archive is designed around utility, not volume for its own sake.

How stories are written

Each article aims to answer four questions quickly: what changed, why it matters, who should care, and what to click next. That keeps the newsroom readable even when the official source is very long.

Trust model

Stories are grounded in official posts or official Diablo IV pages. When information is not officially confirmed, the site should say so clearly instead of presenting guesses as fact.

Good To Know

This site is a fan-made resource hub, not an official Blizzard product. Editorial summaries are meant to improve clarity, not replace the original source.
