Deep Dive

Nightmare Dungeons

How Nightmare Sigils Work

Nightmare Dungeons are harder versions of regular dungeons accessed via Nightmare Sigils. Each sigil shows its tier, dungeon name, and all Dungeon Affixes before you enter. Dungeon Affixes remix gameplay — they buff all monsters in specific ways, add dangerous mechanics, or change dungeon rules entirely.

  • Dungeon Affixes are fixed per sigil — always 1 positive affix + 2–4 negative affixes
  • Monster levels scale with sigil tier
  • Die too many times and you're forced out — the sigil is lost
  • Bloodied Sigils (S12) add the Relentless Butcher affix — always harder than the listed tier

Sigil Types

Sigil Type World Tier Tier Range Monster Levels Revives
Sacred Sigil World Tier III Tiers 1–20 54–73 12
Ancestral Sigil World Tier IV Tiers 21–100 74–154 4

Sigils drop from Tree of Whispers caches, world bosses, events, and other Nightmare Dungeons. Craft new sigils at the Occultist using Sigil Powder (break down unwanted sigils). Sigil Powder is shared across all characters on the same realm.

Access & Difficulty Scaling

  1. Complete the campaign
  2. Complete Cathedral of Light Capstone on Veteran → unlock World Tier III
  3. Farm 10 Grim Favors at the Tree of Whispers → get your first Tier 1 Nightmare Sigil
  4. Progress through tiers as you get stronger

Escalating Nightmares (Season 9+)

A chain of 3 back-to-back Nightmare Dungeons using an Escalation Sigil. Each dungeon inherits and stacks affixes from the previous. After clearing all 3, you fight Astaroth's Lair (starting in Torment I). High risk, high reward.

Rewards: Ancestral Legendaries, Uniques, XP, Gold, Materials.

Positive Affixes

  • + Gold Find — +30% gold
  • + Ancestral Awakening — +10% XP
  • + Horadric Reserves — spawns loot chests
  • + Equipment Delve — equipment cache
  • + Power Shrines — extra shrines
  • + Forgotten Wisdom — XP on kill + well
  • + Treasure Breach — gilded gremlins
  • + Horadric Strongroom — guaranteed strongroom
  • + Chaos Rifts — guaranteed rifts

Negative Affixes

  • − Avenger — enrages nearby monsters
  • − Blood Blister — exploding blood pools
  • − Drifting Shade — chasing explosion
  • − Elemental Totems — buff totems
  • − Empowered Elites — dangerous affixes
  • − Monster Barrier — 85% Max Life as Barrier
  • − Nightmare Portal — random spawns
  • − Stormbane's Wrath — chase orb pulses
  • − Volcanic — flame eruptions

XP vs Gear Dungeons

XP: Forgotten Wisdom / Ancestral Awakening affixes + high-density dungeons

Gear: Equipment Delve / Treasure Breach + Tortured Gift of Mysteries in Helltide

Zone Event

Helltide

How Helltide Works

Helltides are region-wide events lasting ~1 hour, with a ~5-minute gap between events. The sky turns red, demonic activity surges, and monsters are higher level than the player. Monsters drop Aberrant Cinders — a special currency for opening Helltide chests.

⏱️

Timing & Zones

  • ~60 minutes active, ~5 minutes downtime
  • Random regions across the open world
  • Look for red sky and red map coverage
  • Use waypoints to travel between regions
  • Key areas: Witchwater, Cenner Rise, The Crucible
⚠️

Threat Bar

  • Tier 1–2: Normal monster spawns
  • Tier 3: Hell-marked — ambush + Hellborne elite
  • Death resets threat to zero
  • Hellworms spawn as threat rises
  • Profane Mindcage: +10 monster levels, +Cinder rate (stackable 3x)
💰

What to Farm

  • Aberrant Cinders → Mystery Chests (Uniques/Mythics)
  • Living Steel → Grigoire summoning
  • Boss materials from Hellborne
  • Experience — fastest 1–60 leveling
  • Baneful Hearts → Blood Maiden / Accursed Rituals

Endgame Loop

The Pit

How The Pit Works

The Pit is an endgame dungeon variant similar to Greater Rifts from Diablo 3. It has 150 tiers of increasing difficulty. Clear enough monsters within 10 minutes to spawn a boss portal, then defeat the boss to unlock the next tier.

  • Unlocked after completing the Rank 2 Capstone (Hellish Descent)
  • Tiers 1–10 auto-unlocked for all characters on your realm
  • Progression is shared across all characters on the same realm
  • Time left = tiers skipped: 4–6 min → +3 tiers; 6+ min → +5 tiers
  • Death penalty: 1st = -30 sec, 2nd = -60 sec, 3rd+ = -90 sec

Mutation Tiers

Pit Tiers Difficulty Notes

Pit Rewards

  • Ancestral Legendary Items
  • Murmuring Obols
  • Glyph XP and upgrade attempts (3 attempts + 1 bonus if no death)
  • Artificer's Tools — dropped by Pit bosses, upgrade Glyphs
  • Masterworking Materials

Pit Pusher Build Qualities

  • High single-target and AoE damage
  • Survivability — death = time penalty
  • Mobility / clear speed — faster = more tiers skipped
  • Sustain — lifesteal, healing, or damage reduction

Season 12 Exclusive

S12 Seasonal Activities

Killstreak System

Directly inspired by Diablo 3's Killstreak system. Stack consecutive kills for bonus rewards and power.

  • Killing multiple enemies quickly builds a Killstreak counter
  • Killstreaks begin after 15 kills within a short time window
  • Timer resets as long as enemies take direct damage (DoTs only count for initial damage)
  • Tiers: Killstreak → Carnage → Devastation → Bloodbath → Massacre
  • When timer expires → massive chunk of Experience + Seasonal Reputation
  • Death during a Killstreak = streak lost

Bloodied Items

New gear type (not craftable) — dropped gear can roll with the Bloodied special affix. Can appear on any normally dropped item including Uniques and Mythics.

  • Rampage (Armor only) — bonuses scale with Killstreak tier
  • Feast (Weapons only) — triggers based on total kills in streak
  • Hunger (Jewelry only) — extra rewards after streak ends, OR enhances bonuses during streak

Examples: Rampage: +7% Movement Speed per Killstreak tier. Feast: Every 25 kills → Berserking for 6 seconds.

Bloodied Sigils

New sigil modifiers for existing activities. Always include the Relentless Butcher affix.

  • Spawn an empowered Relentless Butcher that hunts the player non-stop
  • Even if defeated, he immediately respawns
  • Can have two Butcher encounters at once (Relentless Butcher + normal Butcher)
  • Difficulty = one tier higher than activated on (e.g., Torment I sigil → Torment II difficulty)
  • Guaranteed at least 1 Bloodied Item drop — best seasonal farming method
  • Enemy density is extremely high — great for XP and Reputation

Fresh Meat Currency

Earned by killing enemies as The Butcher in any S12 activity.

  • Slaughterhouses: spend at end to boost rewards
  • Seasonal vendor: spend for Butcher Uniques
  • Progresses the new Reliquary track
