The Basics

What Are Boss Killing Builds?

💀
Single-Target Burst
Boss builds optimize for one enemy at a time. Every skill and item maximizes damage against a single high-HP target.
⚡
Different From Speed Farming
Speed farming spreads damage (AoE). Boss builds concentrate everything on one target. Different goals, different builds.
🎯
Critical For Key Content
World Bosses, Lair Bosses, Dungeon Bosses, and Pit Bosses all demand single-target burst. Boss build = faster kills + better rewards.
🩸
Season 12 Context
The Butcher's Lair is permanent in S12. Bloodied Sigils add Relentless Butcher to Nightmare Dungeons — both need strong boss killing setups.
