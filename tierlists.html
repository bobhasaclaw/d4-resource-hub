Find Your Playstyle

Tier List Categories

Every tier list source covers different playstyles. Here's what each category means and which classes dominate it in S12.

🏆 Endgame Builds

Builds optimized for high-level Nightmare Dungeons, Pit pushing, and late-game content. These builds can handle the hardest content in the game at the highest torments.

Sources: Icy Veins Endgame • D4Builds • Mobalytics
Best in S12: S-Tier — Paladin S-Tier — Spiritborn

⚡ Speed Farming Builds

Builds optimized for fast clearing — XP runs, key farms, Helltide, and nightmare dungeon speed clears. These prioritize mobility and AoE burst over raw single-target power.

Sources: Icy Veins Speed Farming • D4Builds
Best in S12: A-Tier — Spiritborn A-Tier — Rogue A-Tier — Paladin

💀 Boss Killer Builds

Single-target burst builds for World Bosses, Lair Bosses, and Boss Events. These builds sacrifice AoE and mobility for maximum damage output on a single target.

Sources: Icy Veins Boss Killer
Best in S12: A-Tier — Barbarian A-Tier — Paladin B-Tier — Necromancer

📈 Leveling Builds

Best builds for 1–60 campaign and seasonal leveling. These prioritize ease of play, smooth power curve, and survivability over peak endgame performance. You level with these, then switch to an endgame build at 60.

Sources: D4Builds Leveling Guide • Mobalytics
Best in S12: Easiest — Spiritborn A-Tier — Barbarian A-Tier — Sorcerer

Tier Lists Change Every Patch

Diablo 4 tier lists are not static. Every balance patch — which can come every 2–6 weeks — can dramatically shift the meta. A build that was S-tier last month might drop to B-tier after a single skill adjustment.
