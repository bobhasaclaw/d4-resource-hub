Leveling 101

What Are Leveling Builds?

Leveling builds are specially designed skill setups that prioritize fast dungeon and world clearing to reach level 60 as quickly as possible. Unlike endgame builds that require specific Legendary Aspects, Paragon boards, and Mythic Uniques, leveling builds rely on fewer moving parts and easy-to-find gear.

The key differences between leveling and endgame builds come down to simplicity and scaling. A good leveling build has a short, easy-to-execute rotation, scales well with minimal gear, and can handle dense mob packs without dying constantly. Once you hit level 60, you pivot to a proper endgame build — that's when the real gear chase begins.

Season 12 Bonus: The Killstreak system means chaining kills rewards bonus XP and Seasonal Reputation. Builds with strong AoE and mobility — like Spiritborn Quill Volley or Barbarian HotA — naturally chain more kills and farm faster.

Know Your Path

Campaign vs Season Leveling

How you level depends on whether it is your first character or your 10th alt.

📜

Campaign Leveling

First Playthrough

For your very first character in a fresh realm, you play through Diablo 4's story campaign. This is the intended experience — enjoy it, do not rush it.

  • Focus on story completion and world exploration
  • Use whatever gear drops — do not worry about optimization
  • Do not stress about builds — just get through the story
  • Target: Level 1–50 by campaign end
  • Unlock waypoints as you go for faster travel
  • Do side dungeons for skill points if you are under-levelled
Tip: If you are struggling on story bosses, look for better gear on the World Boss schedule or grind a few Nightmare Dungeons for Ancestral items.
⚡

Seasonal Leveling

Alt Characters / Repeat

Once you have completed the campaign on one character, every seasonal alt should skip it entirely using the Season Journey skip and speed-run to 60.

  • Use the Season Journey skip (completes campaign objectives automatically)
  • Go straight to World Tier dungeons after the intro
  • Use a dedicated leveling build (one of the builds on this page)
  • Target: Level 1–60 in 3–5 hours (top players)
  • Focus on Helltides for dense XP and Bloodied Item drops
  • Join parties for +25% XP bonus
S12 Bonus: Killstreak XP bonuses stack in Helltide — chain kills in dense zones for massive bonus experience. Bloodied Items also start dropping from the first level.

Pro Tips

Leveling Tips — Get to 60 Faster

Small optimizations that add up to hours saved over your leveling sessions.

01

Party Up for +25% XP

Joining even one other player gives a +25% XP bonus to all kills. The bonus is shared, so 4-player parties are ideal for Helltide grinding — split up to cover more ground but regroup for boss events and chest runs.

02

Tree of Whispers — Grim Favors

Do Tree of Whispers cache runs (3–5 dungeons, ~20 min). Each completed Whisper gives a cache with XP, gold, and Obols. Not the absolute fastest method, but reliable and flexible — works at any level.

03

Seasonal Helper Bonus Objectives

The Seasonal Helper shows bonus objectives in each zone — kill X monsters, complete Y events. These give bonus Seasonal Reputation and XP. Always check your map for active bonuses before grinding.

04

Chain Kills with S12 Killstreak System

The Killstreak system in S12 rewards chaining kills within a short time window. Killstreak, Carnage, Devastation, Bloodbath, and Massacre each give chunked XP bonuses at the end. Dense mob areas like Helltide and Nightmare Dungeons are best for this.

05

Refresh Seasonal Alts Frequently

The seasonal character bonuses (bonus stash tabs, seasonal rewards) are per-character. Creating a new seasonal character gives you a fresh start with the season mechanics and lets you optimize your route based on what you learned last time.

06

Do Not Invest in Endgame Gear Yet

Do not spend resources tempering or masterworking gear until you are level 60. Every piece you find before 60 will be replaced. Just pick up the highest iLvl items you find and move on — save your materials for your level 60 build.

Season 12 Specific

Leveling in Season of Slaughter

S12 has unique mechanics that directly speed up your 1–60 journey.

💀
Killstreak XP Bonus

Fresh seasonal characters get Season 12 mechanics from level 1. Chaining 15+ kills within a short time window triggers Killstreak, Carnage, Devastation, Bloodbath, and Massacre tiers — each giving a chunked Experience and Seasonal Reputation bonus at the end. High-mobility, high-AoE builds benefit most.

🩸
Bloodied Items from Level 1

Bloodied Items can drop from the start on seasonal characters. These have special affixes that scale with your Killstreak tier — the Rampage, Feast, and Hunger prefixes all work at any level. Early Bloodied drops can carry you through the leveling phase.

🩸
Bloodied Sigils — High Density XP

Bloodied Sigils (S12 Nightmare Sigils) guarantee at least one Bloodied Item drop and have the Relentless Butcher affix — an empowered Butcher that never stops chasing you. The enemy density is extremely high, making these ideal for killstreak chaining and XP farming.

⚔️
Slaughterhouses — Butcher XP Runs

New S12 seasonal dungeons. You play as The Butcher from dungeon start to finish, using his iconic Hook and Furnace Blast abilities. Great for mass XP runs — and you earn Pound of Flesh for the Butcher Lair boss plus Fresh Meat currency.

🏆
Season Journey Rewards

Completing Season Journey ranks 1–5 gives bonus stash tabs, Paragon Points, and Greater caches. Rush through these as fast as possible — the bonus stash space immediately improves your seasonal economy and the caches give level-appropriate gear to speed up your run.

Recommended S12 Leveling Route:
1. Skip campaign via Season Journey — 2. Grind Helltides on Hard/Penitent — 3. Run Nightmare Dungeons with Bloodied Sigils — 4. Chain killstreaks for XP bonuses — 5. Complete Season Journey ranks for stash tabs and gear caches — 6. Hit 60 and pivot to endgame build
