Leveling 101

What Are Leveling Builds?

Leveling builds are specially designed skill setups that prioritize fast dungeon and world clearing to reach level 60 as quickly as possible. Unlike endgame builds that require specific Legendary Aspects, Paragon boards, and Mythic Uniques, leveling builds rely on fewer moving parts and easy-to-find gear.

The key differences between leveling and endgame builds come down to simplicity and scaling. A good leveling build has a short, easy-to-execute rotation, scales well with minimal gear, and can handle dense mob packs without dying constantly. Once you hit level 60, you pivot to a proper endgame build — that's when the real gear chase begins.

Season 12 Bonus: The Killstreak system means chaining kills rewards bonus XP and Seasonal Reputation. Builds with strong AoE and mobility — like Spiritborn Quill Volley or Barbarian HotA — naturally chain more kills and farm faster.

Spiritborn — Fastest Leveling Class

Quill Volley Spiritborn S-Tier Leveling

The premier leveling build for Spiritborn. Ranged spirit-based attack that fires volleys of quills — melts mob packs quickly while keeping you at safe range. Scales smoothly with any gear.

⚡ Rotation: 1-2-3 spam 🎯 Playstyle: Ranged DPS

Evade Storm Spiritborn S-Tier Leveling

Eagle Spirit infinite evade build. Near-permanent evade window keeps you alive while dealing damage on the move. Also an S-tier endgame build — one build takes you from 1 to 100.

⚡ Rotation: Evade → Damage 🎯 Playstyle: Mobile Hit-and-Run

Barbarian — Simple & Powerful

Hammer of the Ancients (HotA) S-Tier Leveling

The iconic D4 leveling build. Ground Stomp into Hammer of the Ancients — a simple two-button rotation that deals massive AoE damage. Works from level 1 through endgame. Most popular Barbarian leveling build for a reason.

⚡ Rotation: Ground Stomp → HotA 🎯 Playstyle: Melee Burst AoE
📘 D4Builds Guide 📘 Icy Veins Guide
Upheaval Barbarian A-Tier Leveling

Strong single-target + AoE build using Upheaval. Less setup than HotA — just charge resources and cast. Great for players who prefer a more mobile playstyle without relying on Ground Stomp.

⚡ Rotation: Fury Dump → Upheaval 🎯 Playstyle: Mobile Melee
📘 Mobalytics Guide
🗡️

Rogue — High Mobility

Dance of Knives Rogue S-Tier Leveling

The go-to leveling build for Rogue. Fast AoE melee spinner that clears rooms rapidly. Very popular in S12 for its excellent mob-to-mob mobility and satisfying burst. Built by M1PY, one of the top Rogue creators.

⚡ Rotation: Combo Points → Dance of Knives 🎯 Playstyle: Melee Spin-to-Win
📘 M1PY (Mobalytics) 📘 Icy Veins Guide
Rapid Fire Rogue B-Tier Leveling

Safe ranged option for Rogue. Good single-target damage with acceptable AoE. Easier to survive with than Dance of Knives if you prefer keeping distance — better for newer players transitioning to Rogue.

⚡ Rotation: Inner Sight → Rapid Fire 🎯 Playstyle: Ranged Precision
📘 Icy Veins Guide

Necromancer — Pet & Bone Power

Bone Spirit Necromancer S-Tier Leveling

Fastest Necromancer leveling build. Bone Spirit fires a homing missile that one-shots pack leaders and melts groups. Glass-cannon style — very high damage, requires some positioning skill to not die on hard content.

⚡ Rotation: Corpse Explosion → Bone Spirit 🎯 Playstyle: Burst Glass Cannon
📘 Kay (Mobalytics) 📘 Icy Veins Guide
Minion Necromancer A-Tier Leveling

Minion army handles most of the heavy lifting. Golem + skeleton warriors distract enemies while you support from behind. Safer, more relaxed leveling — ideal for players who want to watch Netflix while grinding to 60.

⚡ Rotation: Summon → Support 🎯 Playstyle: Pet / Minion Army
📘 Icy Veins Guide
🔮

Sorcerer — AoE Destruction

Fireball Sorcerer S-Tier Leveling

Classic AoE nuke. Fireball detonates on impact for massive burst damage across dense packs. Very intuitive — point, cast, watch things explode. Scales well with any gear and works all the way through Torment difficulties.

⚡ Rotation: Fireball spam 🎯 Playstyle: Ranged Burst AoE
📘 Icy Veins Guide 📘 D4Builds Leveling
Chain Lightning Sorcerer A-Tier Leveling

Chain Lightning arcs between enemies — exceptional for groups and party play. The bounces hit multiple targets, maximizing the killstreak counter for bonus XP. Great synergy with S12's Killstreak XP bonus system.

⚡ Rotation: Charged Bolts → Chain Lightning 🎯 Playstyle: Ranged Chain AoE
