⚔️ Season 12 Overview

Live Now
Season Name Season of Slaughter
Launched March 11, 2026
Season Type Standard Seasonal Journey
Seasonal Currency Fresh Meat
Season Theme Killstreak System + Bloodied Items + The Butcher
Why It Stands Out

The Season of Slaughter rewards aggressive, chain-kill gameplay. Stack kills to activate Killstreak tiers, equip Bloodied Items that grow stronger with your streak, and unleash The Butcher as your personal mercenary companion.

How S12 Works

Key Mechanics

Six core systems define the Season of Slaughter. Master them all for maximum rewards.

💀
Killstreak System
Stack kills for bonus XP and rewards. Each tier unlocks more power:
  • Killstreak (5 kills)
  • Carnage (15 kills)
  • Devastation (30 kills)
  • Bloodbath (50 kills)
  • Massacre (75+ kills)
🩸
Bloodied Items
World drop-only gear with Killstreak bonuses. Three types:
  • Rampage — Armor pieces
  • Feast — Weapons
  • Hunger — Jewelry

⚠ NOT craftable — only world drops.

Bloodied Sigils
Harder Nightmare Dungeons with the Relentless Butcher affix. Always 1 tier above your current difficulty. Guaranteed to push your build to its limits.
🏚️
Slaughterhouses
Seasonal dungeons where you play as The Butcher. Wield his cleaver, use his abilities, and butcher your way through waves of enemies for bonus Fresh Meat rewards.
🥩
Fresh Meat
S12 seasonal currency. Earned from kills and butchering enemies. Spent at the seasonal vendor for bonus rewards including exclusive cosmetics, Bloodied Item upgrades, and more.
👹
Play as The Butcher
Summon Butcher Shrines in Helltides, access the Butcher's Lair, and enter Slaughterhouses. The legendary D1 villain joins your party as a playable mercenary companion.

New This Season

What's New in S12

Fresh content and collaborations in the Season of Slaughter.

⚔️
5 New Unique Items
Three items themed around The Butcher: his Seals, his Chains, and his Cleaver. All tied to the Killstreak system and designed to push damage to extreme levels.
💀
DOOM: The Dark Ages Collaboration
Diablo x DOOM crossover content. Earn DOOM-themed cosmetics and weapons by completing in-game challenges tied to The Dark Ages expansion.
⚔️
Butcher PvP Event
Butcher-themed PvP event in Fields of Hatred. Face off against other players while the Relentless Butcher roams the zone. Exclusive rewards for top performers.
🏚️
Butcher's Lair — Permanent
The Butcher's Lair is now a permanent addition to the game. Challenge The Butcher solo or in a group for unique legendary drops and the ultimate test of your build.
