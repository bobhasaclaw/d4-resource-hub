Season 12 Overview

⚔️ Season of Slaughter

Live Since March 11, 2026
Season NameSeason of Slaughter
LaunchedMarch 11, 2026
TypeStandard Seasonal Journey
Seasonal CurrencyFresh Meat
ThemeKillstreak System, Bloodied Items, The Butcher

The Season of Slaughter introduces a killstreak mechanic that rewards aggressive play — the more enemies you chain together, the more powerful your Bloodied Items become. Plus, The Butcher from D1 joins you as a fully playable companion.

How It Works

Season 12 Key Mechanics

Everything you need to know about the new seasonal systems.

💀

Killstreak System

Stack enemy kills for bonus XP and rewards.

Killstreak → Carnage → Devastation → Bloodbath → Massacre

Higher killstreaks = better rewards and Bloodied Item scaling.

🩸

Bloodied Items

World drop-only gear with Killstreak-powered bonuses.

Rampage (Armor)Feast (Weapons)Hunger (Jewelry)

NOT craftable. Only world drops.

🩸🗡️

Bloodied Sigils

Harder Nightmare Dungeons with Relentless Butcher affix. Always 1 tier above current difficulty.

🏚️

Slaughterhouses

Seasonal dungeons where you play AS The Butcher. Control his Cleaver and Chains.

🥩

Fresh Meat

S12 seasonal currency from kills and butchering. Spent at seasonal vendor.

👹

Play as The Butcher

The Butcher joins as companion via Butcher Shrines in Helltides, Butcher's Lair, Slaughterhouses.
