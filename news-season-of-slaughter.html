Season of Slaughter launch guide

Blizzard confirmed that Season of Slaughter begins on March 11, 2026 at 10 a.m. PT. The big headline is that players can transform into The Butcher while chasing Bloodied Items and long kill streaks.

📅 March 11, 2026
👹 Butcher powers
🩸 Bloodied Items
⚔️ Free Paladin trial

TL;DR

Blizzard’s official announcement makes this the most important current story for active players. Season of Slaughter goes live on March 11, 2026, introduces a Butcher transformation fantasy, and centers its progression around Kill Streaks and Bloodied Items. There is also a limited free Paladin trial from March 11 to March 18.

What changed

The season announcement positions aggressive chaining and momentum as the defining play pattern. Blizzard says players will rampage through activities as The Butcher, build up kill streak rewards, and chase season-specific gear that scales with that brutality-focused loop.

The result is a season built around speed, pressure, and reward bursts rather than slow defensive play. That matters because it shifts what users should value when they open your build, tier list, and farming pages.

Why it matters

This is the kind of update that changes what a “good” build feels like. Players will care more about uptime, clear speed, mobility, and the ability to keep streaks going cleanly. That means your strongest internal follow-up links are the ones that help people react immediately:

  • season info for mechanic context

What players should notice first

The most immediately useful details in the official post are the launch timing, the Butcher fantasy hook, and the Paladin free-play window. The free Paladin access gives curious users a low-friction way to try a class without committing long-term, which is a strong onboarding opportunity for the site’s class and build pages.

Blizzard also notes that Patch 2.6.0 is available for early download on March 9. That kind of timing detail is exactly the sort of practical note that belongs in your newsroom because it helps people prepare instead of just consuming hype.
