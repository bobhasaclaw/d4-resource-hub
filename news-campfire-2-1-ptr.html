TL;DR

Blizzard’s Campfire recap is valuable because it sits between raw patch documentation and full release coverage. It helps players understand the 2.1 PTR direction in a more readable format than straight notes alone.

Why Campfire recaps matter

Not every player wants to watch a full VOD, and not every patch note explains intent clearly. Campfire recap posts matter because they preserve the framing Blizzard used when talking directly to players. That makes them strong archive material for a site like this.

What this tells users

A Campfire recap usually signals which systems Blizzard thought needed extra explanation, which questions the community was already asking, and which parts of a PTR were likely to draw attention or controversy. That context is often more useful than a plain changelog once time has passed.
