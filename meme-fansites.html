Page Note

How This Section Is Curated

The pages listed here are independent fan-made projects. They are included because they add personality, humor, or community value to the Diablo 4 scene, not because every joke, opinion, or tone choice reflects the editorial voice of D4 Resource Hub.

ℹ️
Independent Fan Projects
These links point to unofficial fan pages and creator-driven projects. They are separate from the main hub and speak in their own voice.
🧭
Curated For Value, Not Full Endorsement
Inclusion here means a page feels worth sharing with the community. It does not mean every line, joke, or opinion on an external page is a formal endorsement by D4 Resource Hub.
🎭
Tone May Be More Playful
Fansites and meme pages often use a lighter or more exaggerated tone than the main site. That difference is part of why this lane exists.
