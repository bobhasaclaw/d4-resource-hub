Mother’s Blessing week guide

Mother’s Blessing is one of the clearest “log in now” events Blizzard runs. The value comes from bonus Tree caches plus faster experience and gold at the same time.

📅 March 18–25, 2025
📈 35% XP
💰 50% Gold
🌳 Bonus Tree caches
Published March 13, 2025

TL;DR

Blizzard’s official event post says that from March 18–25, 2025, players would receive an extra Gift of the Tree cache when turning in Grim Favors, plus 35% more experience and 50% more gold across both Seasonal and Eternal realms.

Why this event is so valuable

Short-term events often look minor until you translate them into player behavior. Mother’s Blessing is not just a small buff weekend. It is a clean acceleration event for leveling alts, finishing the Season Journey, grinding Tree turn-ins, and stockpiling gold while everything stacks together.

That is exactly the sort of event a good resource hub should surface clearly, because users often benefit more from a well-timed boost than from reading another general guide.

What Blizzard confirmed

The official post says the bonus applies to all players, all World Tiers, and both Seasonal and Eternal Realms. It also says the experience bonus stacks, so players can combine it with Elixirs and the Urn of Aggression for even faster progress.

Blizzard specifically frames the week as a chance to climb Levels and World Tiers more quickly, push the Battle Pass, complete the Season Journey, and level multiple characters.

How a player should use it

If the event returns, the best use case is simple: prioritize Tree of Whispers loops, bring an alt or under-geared seasonal character, and stack every available XP multiplier. This is not the moment for slow exploration. It is the moment for route efficiency.
