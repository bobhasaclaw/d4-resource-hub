Pro Tips

How to Get the Most Out of D4 Creators

🎁

Drops-Enabled Streams = Free Loot

Several D4 Partner streamers offer drops-enabled broadcasts — you earn free in-game items just by watching. Enable Twitch drops in your Battle.net account settings, then watch Rob2628 or Snuggie during your play sessions to passively accumulate loot.

🗺️

d4builds.gg is the Standard Build Reference

Rob2628's D4Builds.gg is widely considered the community standard for D4 build planning. It features an interactive planner, seasonal cheat sheet, loot tables, and a desktop overlay app — bookmark it and check it before any seasonal push.

🗞️

Rhykker for Comprehensive Coverage

If you only follow one creator, follow Rhykker. His channel covers builds, tier lists, mechanics, news, and reviews consistently. He's the best all-rounder for staying on top of the D4 meta without having to hunt across multiple sources.

⚙️

MacroBioBoi for Understanding the "Why"

Most creators tell you what to build. MacroBioBoi explains why it works — itemization breakpoints, stat scaling math, speedrun optimizations. Watch his videos when you want to genuinely understand D4 mechanics rather than just copying a build blindly.
