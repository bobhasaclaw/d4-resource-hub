TL;DR

Blizzard’s official Lord of Hatred page confirms a release date of April 28, 2026, a new campaign centered on Mephisto, the region of Skovos, and broader updates touching skills, items, and endgame systems.

The clean read on the expansion

The official page is broad rather than technical, but the direction is clear. Blizzard is presenting Lord of Hatred as a full campaign and systems expansion, not just a class pack or seasonal add-on. That matters because users will expect more than story content from it.

What is safely confirmed

  • launch date: April 28, 2026
  • new campaign built around Mephisto and a final reckoning tone
  • new region: Skovos
  • Paladin is available now via pre-purchase, and Blizzard has separately confirmed Warlock launches with the expansion
  • broader skill, item, and endgame updates
