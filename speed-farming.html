The Basics

What Is Speed Farming?

Speed farming is about clearing content FAST — maximizing your XP/hour, gold/hour, and dungeon runs/hour. It differs from boss killing (single-target) or endgame push runs (high difficulty).

Speed farmers run lower-tier content very quickly, trading raw difficulty for volume. A Spiritborn clearing T40 in 90 seconds out-XP's a class taking 5 minutes on T60. The game is throughput and mobility.

In Season 12, the Killstreak system amplifies speed farming. Chain kills across rapid clears to build tiers (Killstreak → Carnage → Devastation → Bloodbath → Massacre), each awarding massive XP and reputation.

Pro Tips

S12 Speed Farming Tips

Quick habits that make farming routes smoother, denser, and more profitable over long sessions.

01

Keep The Killstreak Alive

Chain kills aggressively for bonus XP while speed farming. Your route should always favor dense packs over slightly higher difficulty if it keeps the streak moving.

02

Bloodied Sigils Beat Slower Dungeons

Bloodied Sigils are one of the best S12 loop options because they stay fast, keep the action dense, and feed guaranteed seasonal drops.

03

Use Slaughterhouses For Burst Value

Slaughterhouses pay off when your build can burst Butcher targets quickly. Work them into your route instead of treating them like standalone content.

04

Party Bonus Is Free Efficiency

Join a group for the +25% XP bonus whenever possible. Split efficiently, cover more ground, and regroup around high-value events or boss windows.

05

Tree of Whispers Is A Great Gold Reset

Use Whisper caches when your main loop starts feeling thin on gold. They are short, flexible, and easy to slot between denser farming runs.

06

Spend Obols With Intent

Use the Purveyor of Curiosities to target cheap upgrade slots and bridge weaker gear pieces instead of scattering Obols randomly across your whole setup.
