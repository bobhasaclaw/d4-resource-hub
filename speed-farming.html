The Basics

What Is Speed Farming?

Speed farming is about clearing content FAST — maximizing your XP/hour, gold/hour, and dungeon runs/hour. It differs from boss killing (single-target) or endgame push runs (high difficulty).

Speed farmers run lower-tier content very quickly, trading raw difficulty for volume. A Spiritborn clearing T40 in 90 seconds out-XP's a class taking 5 minutes on T60. The game is throughput and mobility.

In Season 12, the Killstreak system amplifies speed farming. Chain kills across rapid clears to build tiers (Killstreak → Carnage → Devastation → Bloodbath → Massacre), each awarding massive XP and reputation.

Pro Tips

S12 Speed Farming Tips

  • Killstreak System: Chain kills for bonus XP while speed farming — build your streak before the timer expires
  • Bloodied Sigils: Run faster than regular Nightmare Dungeons with guaranteed Bloodied Item drops
  • Slaughterhouses: Bonus XP for Butcher kills — play as The Butcher for Fresh Meat currency
  • Party Bonus: Join a party for +25% XP bonus when speed farming with others
  • Tree of Whispers: Use caches for gold — efficient 10-15 minute activity
  • Purveyor of Curiosities: Gamble for cheap item rolls to find upgrades
