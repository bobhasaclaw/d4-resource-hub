OpenClaw Review Flow

How Reports Turn Into Approved Work

OpenClaw should act like an organizer and summarizer, not an auto-deployer. This keeps the quality gate intact while still making feedback fast to review.

1
Collect The Report
Watch the feedback channels and capture the title, category, problem summary, affected page, and suggested fix.
2
Normalize It
Repost a clean review summary so every report has the same format and the same minimum detail before it reaches you.
3
Wait For Approval
Use simple review states: new, reviewing, approved, not now, and shipped.
4
Turn Approval Into Work
Once approved, the summary becomes a clear implementation task for the website. No silent changes. No skipped review step.
