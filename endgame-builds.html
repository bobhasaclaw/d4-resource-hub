The Basics

What Are Endgame Builds?

Endgame builds are character setups optimized for high-difficulty content after reaching level 60. Unlike leveling builds that prioritise rapid progression through the campaign, endgame builds are engineered for survival, damage output, and consistency in the most demanding activities Diablo 4 offers.

These activities include Nightmare Dungeons (T50+), The Pit for Paragon XP and Glyph upgrades, high-level Helltides, Lair Bosses, World Bosses, and Infernal Hordes. A strong endgame build balances four pillars: damage, survival, mobility, and consistency.

⚡ Key Difference from Leveling Builds

Endgame builds assume full access to the Paragon system, Tempering, Masterworking, and Codex Aspects. Gear quality matters enormously, so a T50+ build needs strong Ancestral bases, correct Temper rolls, and an optimized Paragon board to truly perform.

Pro Tips

Endgame Progression Tips

Core principles for efficiently advancing your character into the endgame and pushing high Torment tiers.

01

Start with a Safe S-Tier Build

Spiritborn or Paladin have the highest ceiling and are easiest to pilot. Pick a proven S-tier build from the tier list and commit before branching out.

02

Paragon Points First

Unlock and fill your Paragon nodes before heavy gear investment. Paragon provides the biggest stat gains early in endgame.

03

Temper Before Masterworking

Always affix Temper gear before investing Masterworking materials. Tempering provides targeted power that Masterworking amplifies.

04

Focus on One Build

Don't alternate between multiple builds. Pick one endgame build, gather its required Aspects and Uniques, then optimize it to the point where upgrades become obvious.

05

Codex Aspects Are Free Power

Extract valuable Aspects from Legendaries and imprint them onto better bases. Codex powers scale indefinitely with your item level.

06

Prioritize Greater Affixes

Items with Greater Affixes are vastly more powerful than standard rolls. Build around three to four Greater Affixes in your core gear slots before chasing luxury upgrades.
