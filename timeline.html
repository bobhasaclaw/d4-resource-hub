Pattern Analysis

Season Patterns

Cadence & Duration

  • Seasons run approximately every 3–4 months
  • Average season length: ~12 weeks (3 months)
  • Typical gap between seasons: ~1–2 weeks
  • Season start day: usually a Tuesday
  • Shorter seasons (~9 weeks) happen when expansions launch (S5 before VoH, S8)
  • Longer seasons (~15 weeks) happen over holiday periods (S3, S6)

S13 Projection

  • Expected Start: April 28, 2026 (Lord of Hatred launch)
  • Pattern: Blizzard consistently aligns major season launches with expansion releases
  • Vessel of Hatred launched alongside S6 (October 2024)
  • Lord of Hatred confirmed for April 28, 2026 — most logical S13 trigger
  • Season Name: TBA (Blizzard announces ~2 weeks before launch)
