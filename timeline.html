Season 1 — Season 12

Every D4 Season

S12 Season of Slaughter
Current

March 11, 2026 — Ongoing

Key Mechanic: Killstreak system (stack kills for bonus XP/rewards) + Bloodied Items (Killstreak-powered gear) + Bloodied Sigils (Relentless Butcher affix).

Standout: Play as The Butcher! Slaughterhouse Dungeons, Shrines of Slaughter in Helltides, PvP event in Fields of Hatred. Fresh Meat seasonal currency. DOOM: The Dark Ages collaboration.

🎯 Killstreaks 🩸 Bloodied Items 👹 Play as The Butcher 💀 DOOM Collab
S11 Divine Intervention
Dec 11, 2025 — Mar 11, 2026

December 11, 2025 — March 11, 2026 (~13 weeks)

Key Mechanic: Divine Gifts + Sanctification — divine-themed borrowed power system.

Standout: Shorter season (~3 months). Leads directly into S12 and the pre-Lord of Hatred content push. New Uniques and class items added.

✨ Divine Gifts ⛪ Sanctification
S10 Infernal Chaos
Sep 23 — Dec 11, 2025

September 23 — December 11, 2025 (~11 weeks)

Key Mechanic: Chaos Rifts + Chaos Powers — rift-like activity with chaos-themed seasonal powers.

Standout: New Nightmare Dungeon activities. Season Journey and Season Blessings updates. Keyboard and mouse support added for console players.

🌀 Chaos Rifts ⚡ Chaos Powers
S9 Sins of the Horadrim
Jul — Sep 23, 2025

July — September 23, 2025 (~11 weeks)

Key Mechanic: Horadric Spells — Catalysts + Infusions + Arcana system. One of D4's most complex seasonal mechanics.

Standout: Horadric Jewels (socketable jewels with tradeoffs). Horadric Strongrooms (micro-dungeons). Dungeon Escalations culminating in an Astaroth boss fight. Praised for depth.

📜 Horadric Spells 💎 Horadric Jewels 🏚️ Strongrooms
S8 Belial's Return
Apr 29 — Jul 2025

April 29 — July 2025 (~9 weeks)

Key Mechanic: Binding Signature skills — special seasonal skills tied to the Belial theme.

Standout: Belial, Lord of Lies, returns as a major story antagonist. New Belial boss encounter. New seasonal questline.

🎭 Belial Boss ⛓️ Signature Skills
S7 Witchcraft
Jan 21 — Apr 29, 2025

January 21 — April 29, 2025 (~14 weeks)

Key Mechanic: Witch Powers + Occult Gems — dark witchcraft-themed borrowed power system. Age of Hatred Unchained storyline.

Standout: Cursed Chests seasonal treasure. Continued Mercenaries, Runewords, and Dark Citadel content from VoH.

🧙 Witch Powers 💜 Occult Gems 📦 Cursed Chests
S6 Hatred Rising
Expansion Required

October 8, 2024 — January 21, 2025 (~15 weeks)

Key Mechanic: Vessel of Hatred expansion launch. New class, new region, new systems.

Standout: Spiritborn class (Nahantu jungle). Mercenaries system. Runewords crafting. Dark Citadel (4-player co-op dungeon). Kurast Undercity. Level cap raised to 60. New Torment difficulty tier.

🏝️ Nahantu Region 🦅 Spiritborn ⚔️ Runewords 🛡️ Mercenaries
S5 Infernal Hordes
Aug 6 — Oct 8, 2024

August 6 — October 8, 2024 (~9 weeks)

Key Mechanic: Infernal Hordes — wave-based structured endgame activity with escalating demons, mini-bosses, and an Infernal boss.

Standout: Testbed for what became a core endgame loop. New Boss Difficulty Tiers for existing World Bosses. Ran alongside VoH lead-up.

🔥 Infernal Hordes 👹 Boss Tiers
S4 Loot Reborn
May 14 — Aug 7, 2024

May 14 — August 7, 2024 (~12 weeks)

Key Mechanic: Armory system — save and swap between multiple build loadouts with one click. Complete itemization overhaul.

Standout: Widely considered the "rescue season." Tax Collector NPC. Massive QoL improvements. Legendary gem upgrades simplified. Paragon system improvements. Massive positive reception.

📦 Armory System 💰 Tax Collector ⚡ Itemization Overhaul
S3 The Construct
Jan 23 — May 14, 2024

January 23 — May 14, 2024 (~15 weeks)

Key Mechanic: Seneschal Construct companion — AI companion that collects loot, damages enemies, and provides buffs. Customizable with Incense and Artifacts.

Standout: Hazard Vaults (Construct-themed puzzle dungeons). Ladders (Seasonal Leaderboards) added for the first time. Construct became a permanent feature.

🤖 Seneschal Construct 🏗️ Hazard Vaults 📊 Ladders
S2 Blood
Oct 17, 2023 — Jan 23, 2024

October 17, 2023 — January 23, 2024 (~14 weeks)

Key Mechanic: Blood Merchants + Blood Dawn world events — contribute blood to a shared pool, triggering bonus events with extra rewards.

Standout: Seneschal Binding (Construct companion) introduced as a teaser. Wolf companion in the battle pass. New Legendary Aspects and item balancing.

🩸 Blood Merchants 🐺 Wolf Companion 🔗 Construct (Tease)
S1 Malignance
Jul 20 — Oct 17, 2023

July 20 — October 17, 2023 (~13 weeks)

Key Mechanic: Malignant Hearts — socketable seasonal gems granting powerful bonuses when combined (Diablo III's Legacy of Dreams style).

Standout: Launch season. 5 classes (Barbarian, Druid, Necromancer, Rogue, Sorcerer). Return of The Butcher as an endgame boss. Alchemist NPC for rerolling item powers.

🐕 Maligator Pet 💎 Malignant Hearts 👹 The Butcher

📅 Season Pattern

⏱️

Every 3–4 Months

Seasons run approximately every 12 weeks (3 months). Some seasons run slightly shorter (~9 weeks) or longer (~15 weeks).

📆

Typical Start Day

Seasons typically begin on a Tuesday. ~1–2 weeks of interlude between seasons.

🚀

S13 Expected

April 28, 2026 — aligned with Lord of Hatred expansion launch. Expansions have consistently triggered new seasons simultaneously.

Average season length: ~12 weeks  |  Expansions: ~yearly  |  Current: S12 (Slaughter)
