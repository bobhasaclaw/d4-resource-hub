TL;DR

Blizzard says Warlock will launch with Lord of Hatred on April 28, 2026. The official framing is clear: this is a class built around commanding demonic forces, hellish energies, and forbidden power rather than holy or elemental themes.

What is officially confirmed

The reveal confirms the class name, fantasy, and release timing. Blizzard describes Warlock as a visceral caster that summons demonic legions and weaponizes Hell against Hell. That gives your site enough confirmed ground to support Warlock coverage without filling in blanks from speculation.

Why this matters for the site

Warlock is the kind of reveal that changes multiple parts of the hub at once. It affects class comparisons, tier lists, the videos page, and the long-term structure of guides and build pages. The key is to separate confirmed information from future expectations so users trust the page.

For now, the cleanest editorial move is to treat Warlock as a confirmed upcoming class with a known release date, a known fantasy, and not much more unless Blizzard says more.

Useful player detail

Blizzard also notes that pre-purchase items tied to the Warlock start showing up before the full expansion launch, while the class itself remains locked to the expansion release. That matters because it helps explain why people may see Warlock cosmetics and discussion before they can actually play the class.
