Gilded Goblins event guide

March of the Goblins was more than a loot novelty. Blizzard used it to add six goblin variants that stayed in the game after the event ended.

📅 March 4–11, 2025
👺 6 new variants
⭐ Reputation rewards
🔥 Permanent additions
Published February 27, 2025

TL;DR

Blizzard’s official post says March of the Goblins ran from March 4 to March 11, 2025, introduced six new Treasure Goblin variants, tied progress to a reputation track, and left the new goblin variants in the game after the event ended.

Why this event stood out

Most live events boost drop rates for a short time and then disappear. This one mattered more because it permanently expanded the goblin ecosystem. Blizzard says the new variants would remain in all realms even after the increased activity ended, which means the event changed the long-term game, not just one week of it.

The most useful mechanics Blizzard confirmed

The official post says players could encounter larger Treasure Goblin packs, more Greed Shrines, and more Treasure Goblins during the Avarice world boss while the event was live. That immediately changed route planning and made the event worth targeting if your goal was quick loot bursts rather than slower steady farming.

Blizzard also tied the Goblin Statue in central Kyovashad to the event’s reputation track, which gave the whole system a clearer chase structure than a simple “more goblins are out” notice.

The six goblin variants mattered

The official post names six new variants, each built around a different reward lane: crafting materials, gold, runes and gem fragments, obols, legendary piles, and even the ultra-rare Fancy Old Fedric with a Mythic Unique and Resplendent Spark. That reward specificity is what made the event feel more strategic than random.
